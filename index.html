SOSTENA NTABO'S 5 POINT AGENDA
I SOSTENA will labour to achieve the following if elected.

  • ✅ Embrace servant leadership, by being readily available to serve members at all times.
  • ✅Embrace arbitration in resolution of work related disputes.
  • ✅Coordinate the creation of a KUPPET KISII APP, union website and KUPPET Kisii strategic plan.
  • ✅Push to ensure more members are co-sponsored to professional trainings such as KNEC, KEMI, professional Guidance and counseling etc.
  • ✅Champion for establishments of union labour education days and championing the needs of our teachers with disabilities.
