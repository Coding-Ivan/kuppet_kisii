About Us

Vision

A strong National progressive post primary teachers’ Union in Kisii County where justice, service and integrity are upheld to enhance cohesion and conciliation


Mission


To unite all teachers in post primary institutions in Kisii County by advocating for the establishment of sound ethical and professional policies that guarantee job security and fair solutions to members’ grievances through lawful union activities, affiliation, negotiation and research.


Our Core Values


Service, Justice and Integrity.
